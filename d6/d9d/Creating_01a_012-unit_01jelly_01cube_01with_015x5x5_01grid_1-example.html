Create a volumetric jelly cube entity for soft body simulationCreates a 3D grid of vertices (gridSizeÂ³ total) with surface faces for rendering. Unlike CreateCube (which has only surface vertices), this creates internal vertices needed for volumetric soft body simulation ("jelly" effect).

The spacing between vertices is automatically calculated as: size / (gridSize - 1)
