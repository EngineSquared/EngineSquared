Enumerator
Silent 

Fail silently.

Nothing 

Let the exception propagate.

LogAndContinue 

Just log the error and resume execution.

LogAndFinishScheduler 

Log the error, run the next systems and stop execution of other schedulers.

LogAndStop 

Log the error and stop execution.
